## 设计模式入门
## Design Pattern Getting Started.

<br>

#### Some Design Principles
#### 一些设计原则
<br>

- 找出应用中可能需要变化之处，把它们独立出来，不要和那些不需要变化的代码混在一起。
- Identify the aspects of your application that very and separate them from what stays the same.

<br>

- 针对接口编程（针对超类型编程），而不是针对实现编程。
- Program to an interface (program to supertype), not an implementation.

<br>

- 多用组合，少用继承。
- Favor composition over inheritance.
